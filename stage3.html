Stage 3: Real-World Applications of AI

1. AI in Healthcare

AI is used for disease detection, drug discovery, and personalized medicine. Machine learning models analyze medical images, detect abnormalities, and assist doctors in diagnosing diseases.

2. AI in Finance

AI-driven algorithms help detect fraud, automate trading, and provide personalized financial advice using predictive analytics.

3. AI in Transportation

Self-driving cars, traffic prediction, and route optimization leverage AI to improve efficiency and safety in transportation systems.

4. AI in Entertainment

Streaming platforms like Netflix and YouTube use AI for content recommendation, enhancing user experience with personalized suggestions.
